I write as a student who is still learning, and these posts are a way to collect what I study and to share it with others.

- weekly posts aimed to anyone. In particular, my goal is to develope all the theory needed to give a complete proof of the Christmas Theorem, starting from the very basics.

Everything here is written in my free time, so please keep in mind that there may be occasional errors.

## Projects

### Route to the Fermat's Christmas Theorem
A long-form series of short weekly notes developing most of the theory needed to give a complete proof of the Fermat's Christmas Theorem, starting from the very basics. I will try to write the posts in a very simple language, so that anyone could easily keep up with reading it.

### Advanced Notes
Posts on more advanced topics that I encounter along the way (ideas that I like, want to remember, or want to understand better).
