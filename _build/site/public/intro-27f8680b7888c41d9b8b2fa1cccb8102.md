Hi! I am a master’s student in mathematics, mostly interested in algebraic stuff.
In this blog I share things I find interesting while I study.

I write as a student who is still learning, and these posts are a way to collect what I study and to share it with others.

This blog will mainly have two kinds of projects:

- weekly posts aimed to anyone. In particular, my goal is to develope all the theory needed to give a complete proof of the Christmas Theorem, starting from the very basics.

- posts on more advanced topics that I find along the way (things that I like, or that I want to understand better).

Everything here is written in my free time, so please keep in mind that there may be occasional errors.
